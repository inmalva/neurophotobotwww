Refund & Cancellation Policies

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Service owner: Malva Informatica S.L.

Country of registration: Spain

Contacts: https://t.me/ruslan_comuna_support, support@neurophotobot.app

1. General Provisions

This policy governs the terms of refunds and cancellations for digital services provided through NeuroPhoto Bot.

All offered services are digital, and refunds are issued partially depending on which stages of service delivery have been completed.

By making a payment, the user confirms acceptance of this policy.

2. Refund Policy

2.1. Refund after Model Training

If the process of training a personal AI model has not yet started, the user may request a full refund.

If the training process has already been launched and successfully completed, €3 will be deducted from the total payment — this amount covers the computational resources used to train the model.

2.2. Refund after Image Generations

A fee of €0.10 (ten cents) is deducted for each generated image.

Example: if the user trained a model (€3) and created 50 images, the total deduction will be: €3 (training) + €5 (50 × €0.10) = €8.

2.3. Refund for Subscription

If the user purchased an annual subscription, a refund is available proportionally to the number of unused full months.

If the subscription was used for more than one month, the refund amount is calculated as follows:

The total cost of the subscription minus the payment for the used full months.

Example: annual subscription €200, used for 2.5 months → deduction: 3 × €20 = €60, refund amount €140.

2.4. Exceptions

  • Refunds are not available if all generation credits have been used.
  • Refunds are not issued if the user has violated the Terms of Service.
  • Refunds are not possible once the digital service has been fully delivered.

2.5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team via Telegram: https://t.me/ruslan_comuna_support

In your message, please include:

  • your Telegram username;
  • payment date;
  • reason for the refund request.

Requests are reviewed within 5 business days.

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1. Cancellation of Model Training

If the training process has not yet started, the user may cancel the order and receive a full refund.

If the training process has already started or been completed, cancellation is not possible since the service has already been delivered.

3.2. Cancellation of Image Generations

Generation can be canceled only before it starts.

Once the generation is completed, no refund will be issued since the result has already been delivered to the user.

3.3. Cancellation of Subscription

Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their account or by contacting the support team.

After cancellation, the subscription remains active until the end of the paid period, and no further payments are charged.

For early cancellation of an annual subscription, the refund is calculated proportionally to the remaining full months (see Section 2.3).

3.4. Additional Provisions

Cancellation requested after a digital service has started is considered a completed transaction and is not refundable.

The service reserves the right to deny cancellation requests in cases of Terms of Service violations or abuse of the system.
