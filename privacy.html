1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used by NeuroPhoto Bot, a digital service operating within the Telegram Mini Apps environment.

By using our service, you agree to the terms of data processing described below.

Malva Informatica S.L. acts as the data controller in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services:

  • the user's Telegram identifier;
  • photos uploaded by the user for model training and image generation;
  • text prompts used for generating images.

We do not collect IP addresses, geolocation, device information, or any other technical or personal data unrelated to service delivery.

3. How We Use the Data

The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • to train a personalized AI model based on the uploaded photos;
  • to generate images from the user's text prompts;
  • to store generated results within the user's profile inside the application;
  • to ensure proper service functionality and manage payment transactions.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

Uploaded photos and generated results are stored within the user's profile.

Data is retained for as long as the user maintains an active account or until deletion is requested.

Users may request complete data deletion at any time by contacting our support team: https://t.me/ruslan_comuna_support.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

To process images and generate results, the service relies on external artificial intelligence systems.

Data is transferred only to the extent necessary to fulfill the user's request.

We do not control how external AI platforms store or process the transmitted data but carefully select trusted providers that follow recognized standards of data security and confidentiality.

No personal data is shared with third parties, except as required by law.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • all data transmissions are encrypted and performed over secure protocols;
  • access to data is restricted to authorized systems only;
  • photos and prompts are never used to train external AI models or published without user consent.

7. User Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, users have the right to:

  • request a copy of their personal data;
  • request correction or deletion of data;
  • restrict or object to data processing;
  • request data portability;
  • file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team: https://t.me/ruslan_comuna_support

8. Children

The service is intended for users aged 16 and older.

We do not knowingly collect or process personal data from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately — the information will be deleted without delay.

The date of the latest revision is shown at the top of this document.

Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
